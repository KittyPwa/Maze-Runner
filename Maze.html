Instructions

Movement : use the directional arrows

  • 'E' : Use the selected activatable item
  • 'Z' : Cycle to the next activatable item in the inventory
  • 'Spacebar' : Use the activatable entity

Objectives :

  • Get to the end without getting torn apart by the wandering monsters
  • Gather items and grow stronger but beware : grow not arrogant for danger lurks around every corner
  • Gather gold to buy items at the shop, available between maze runs.
